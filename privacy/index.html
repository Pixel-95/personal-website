2. Hosting and Server Log Data

The recipient is, in particular, GitHub, Inc., 88 Colin P Kelly Jr St, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA. Processing in third countries, especially the United States, cannot be ruled out. Further information can be found in the GitHub Privacy Statement.

The specific retention period for technical log data depends on the hosting provider's policies.

3. Local Fonts

The fonts used on this website are loaded locally from this webspace. As a result, simply visiting the site does not establish a connection to Google Fonts or any other external font service for the purpose of loading fonts.

4. Contact by Email

If you contact me by email, I process the information you provide in order to handle and, if necessary, respond to your inquiry. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR insofar as the communication concerns pre-contractual or contractual matters, and otherwise Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

The data is stored for as long as required to process the inquiry or as long as statutory retention obligations apply.

This website does not use a contact form. The email link merely opens your email program or email service.
